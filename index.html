About

Mayor Komba Sam


Leadership. Vision. Progress.
Welcome to the official site of Mayor Komba Sam, the transformative leader of Koidu City, now in his second consecutive term.
Mayor Sam's tenure is marked by significant strides in urban development, education, healthcare, and economic growth, all aimed at improving the quality of life for every resident.
Under his visionary leadership, Koidu City has:
- Modernized infrastructure and public facilities.
- Enhanced educational programs.
- Expanded access to quality healthcare.
- Fostered economic growth and job creation.
Mayor Sam's inclusive and transparent governance style ensures that every voice is heard and every concern addressed. Join us in building a brighter, more prosperous future for Koidu City.
Together, we thrive. Together, we build a better tomorrow.

PEOPLE CENTER POLITICIAN

As a People Center Politician, I embody a unique juxtaposition—a bridge between social equality and a degree of social hierarchy. My political outlook embraces centrism, advocating for a balance that avoids extreme shifts to the left or right. In this delicate equilibrium, I champion pragmatic solutions, compromise, and common ground.

Projects and Initiatives

Here you'll find an overview of key projects and initiatives undertaken during Mayor Komba Sam's tenure, aimed at fostering growth, improving welfare, and strengthening governance in Koidu City.

  • All
  • Growth
  • Welfare
  • Governance

Koeyor New Market Infrastructure

A newly built market to help local sellers grow their business and give shoppers a better place to buy what they need.

D.O Barracks Waste Management

A new waste system was set up to keep the area clean, reduce sickness, and improve how trash is handled.

Koidu City Diamond Clock Tower

A tall clock tower built in the heart of the city to represent Koidu’s pride, growth, and unity.

Modern School Buildings

New school buildings were built to give children a better place to learn and help teachers do their jobs well.

Small Sefadu Court Barry

The community court was improved to make sure people can get fair treatment and solve problems peacefully.

Public toilet constructed at Gbense Market with support from the Kono Diasporas

Clean and safe toilets were added at the market with help from Kono people living abroad, improving hygiene for everyone.

Furniture fabrication distributed to Primary Schools in Koidu City

New desks and chairs were made to help pupils sit and learn better in schools across the city.

His Worship Mayor Komba Sam with MAFFS in the beautification of Koidu City through tree planting

Trees were planted across town to make Koidu greener, cooler, and more beautiful for everyone.

Cash for Work for Koidu Youth

Youth were given paid short-term jobs to help them earn money and learn useful work skills.

Food Support for Flood Victims

Food was provided to families who lost homes or belongings during floods, helping them recover quickly.

Support for Quarry Women

Help was given to hardworking women in stone-breaking areas to improve their income and safety.

Support for Ansarul Girls School

School improvements were made to help more girls get a good education and feel safe while learning.

Yardu Mosques Infrastructure

Renovated mosques to give worshippers better places to pray and hold community activities.

Trash bins for waste collection within Koidu City

Trash bins were placed in key areas to help people throw garbage properly and keep the streets clean.

His Worship planting trees to have a green-Koidu City

Mayor Komba Sam led by example in planting trees to teach others the value of protecting nature.

Donation ceremony of cassava processing machines to Agro Music Empire

Given machines to help local farmers and youth process cassava faster and earn more from their work.

Dorma Road Expansion

Expanded and fixed roads to reduce traffic, prevent accidents, and make it easier to move around.

Moigbedu Bridge Construction

Built a bridge to link neighborhoods and improve access for people and goods.

KissiBona Bridge Construction

A new bridge was built to help people and vehicles cross safely and quickly.

CESSPOOL Acquisition

Special trucks were brought in to clean waste from the city faster and keep the environment healthy.

Threshing & Tractor for Waste Management

Brought in equipment to better manage city waste and support cleaning services.

Face-lifting Faudu Dumpsite facility

Upgraded a major dumpsite to make waste handling safer and reduce pollution risks.

Street beautification through painting mandated by His Worship

Mayor Komba Sam initiated the painting of buildings and sidewalks to brighten up the city.

Mount Zion Road pavement work in Progress

Construction is ongoing to pave this major road, making travel easier and safer once completed.
