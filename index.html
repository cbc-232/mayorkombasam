Leadership. Vision. Progress.
Welcome to the official site of Mayor Komba Sam, the transformative leader of Koidu City, now in his second consecutive term.
Mayor Sam's tenure is marked by significant strides in urban development, education, healthcare, and economic growth, all aimed at improving the quality of life for every resident.
Under his visionary leadership, Koidu City has:
- Modernized infrastructure and public facilities.
- Enhanced educational programs.
- Expanded access to quality healthcare.
- Fostered economic growth and job creation.
Mayor Sam's inclusive and transparent governance style ensures that every voice is heard and every concern addressed. Join us in building a brighter, more prosperous future for Koidu City.
Together, we thrive. Together, we build a better tomorrow.
